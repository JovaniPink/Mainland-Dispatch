## Review boundary

This is an article-candidate audit, not a publication list. A successful HTTP
request or metadata match does not mean the full source was read, corroborated,
or approved. `publisher-canonical` means the publisher exposed a canonical URL;
`redirect-resolved` means the supplied address was normalized to the live
publisher endpoint; `supplied` means the address has not crossed that boundary.

No discovery-page identity, rankings, accounts, metrics, comments, or links are
retained. None of those materials can support a public claim. The candidate
article, primary records, independent corroboration, later developments, and
limitations must all pass review before one article-specific Dispatch may be
created.
