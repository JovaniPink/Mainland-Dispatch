## Critical corrections

- One discussion-only prompt is absent from the catalog by design.
- Tiangong-2 is not the later modular space station.
- A vendor or investigator's cyber attribution remains attributed.
- Gait recognition of enrolled subjects is not universal identification.
- A greenbelt's physical completion does not establish ecological success.
- Promotional missile pricing is not a verified procurement cost.
- Stopping new domain registrations is not automatically a total market exit.
- Capacity installed is not electricity generated.
