## Boundary

This is an intake ledger, not a set of article summaries or Dispatches. A title,
redirect, search result, partial page or publisher statement does not satisfy
the direct-reading and corroboration gate. No popularity, account, ranking,
discussion or comment data is retained.

Every withheld record remains outside the public archive until the full lawful
canonical text, controlling records, independent corroboration, responses,
method limits and later developments have been reviewed. Rejected records map
to no Dispatch. This batch creates no public relationships.

## Critical corrections

- The live Microsoft first-party statement replaces the dead supplied URL, but
  it is one participant's account and cannot corroborate itself.
- The social-post headline's number conflicts with the underlying paper's
  published estimate and may not advance until that discrepancy is resolved.
- “Support” for open access, a weather-modification coverage proposal and a
  demographic-policy announcement are not recorded as implemented outcomes.
- U.S. certification about Hong Kong is attributed to the statutory decision
  maker rather than presented as a neutral measurement.
- Research based on witness testimony, modeled resistance, search samples or
  disinformation attribution retains sampling and method limits.
- A short linked commentary, dead syndication, unsupported national-ban claim
  and unstable video are rejected instead of being inflated into articles.
