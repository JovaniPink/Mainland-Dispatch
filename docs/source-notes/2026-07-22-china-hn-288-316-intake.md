## What this intake does

This batch captures the next 30 user-supplied high-engagement HN submissions as
Desk review records. It preserves the external article URL, HN story identity,
points and comment counts at intake, publication chronology where known, and a
source-specific review warning. HN ranking is discovery metadata only: it does
not show that an article is accurate, representative, current, or suitable for
publication.

The external article and the HN discussion have different roles. The article is
a candidate source that still needs to be read and corroborated. The HN thread
may later provide commentary context after full-thread review. Neither role
creates a verified public artifact.

## Corrections built into the review records

- The 2019 Reuters bitcoin-mining story concerned an NDRC draft catalogue and
  consultation, not an immediate nationwide ban. The later 2021 crackdown is a
  separate event.
- The two social-credit stories risk turning court judgment-defaulter lists,
  travel restrictions, financial credit, local pilots, and other programs into
  a fictional single national score. Review must identify the actual program,
  agency, restriction, denominator, and date.
- The 2017 Tesla story precedes the final 2018 Shanghai agreement. A preliminary
  arrangement cannot inherit later ownership, approval, construction, or
  production facts.
- The March 2022 Tell HN post is a first-person Shanghai snapshot and forecast.
  It is not national epidemiological evidence and must not be rewritten with
  hindsight as if the author knew the later lockdown course.
- The 2026 jet-engine essay is explanatory analysis, not a definitive technical
  source. Its headline cannot support the timeless assertion that China makes
  no jet engines; individual engine programs, materials, manufacturing yields,
  certification, imports, and deployments require separate evidence.
- Healthy life expectancy is a modeled WHO measure, not ordinary life
  expectancy or a general healthcare ranking.
- “A fifth of homes” and “40% of top sellers” are method-dependent estimates.
  Their samples, definitions, denominators, geography, and dates must accompany
  any later number.
- The AP moon-outpost item reports talks, not an agreement or constructed base.
  The obsolete Big Story URL needs a canonical or archived copy.
- The Log4j item must keep vulnerability discovery, vendor notification, the
  regulator's timeline, and the reported suspension of cooperation separate.
- Reporting on Xinjiang, detention, torture, information operations, foreign
  influence, and press restrictions requires especially explicit attribution,
  affected-community perspective, official responses, and later records.

## HN commentary quality rule

No comment was selected in this intake. A later reviewer must inspect the
complete relevant tree, including corrections and disagreement, before keeping
any perspective. A comment is potentially useful only when it offers a
checkable source, a concrete correction, relevant and clearly labeled firsthand
experience, an argument whose assumptions are exposed, or a useful distinction
or uncertainty that changes the next reporting step.

Popularity, confident tone, professional identity, agreement with the article,
or agreement with the editor do not qualify a comment. Exclude jokes, slogans,
national stereotypes, personal attacks, repetition, unsupported geopolitical
claims, and claims that votes represent consensus. Selected ideas should
normally be paraphrased without usernames. HN remains `commentary-context` and
can never supply evidence for an Atlas artifact or factual relationship.
