## Critical corrections

- Resolver selection is not evidence that all phone content was transferred.
- A visa motive remains attributed unless an official record establishes it.
- Cyber exploitation evidence and actor attribution are separate claims.
- GCJ-02 map-coordinate mismatch is not GPS hardware changing position.
- Court-defaulter restrictions, private credit products, local pilots and a
  hypothetical universal score are not interchangeable.
- Export licensing and presumptive denial are not rewritten as an outright ban.
- The 2016 video concerns Tiangong-2, not the later modular space station.
- Six-month modeled emissions do not establish an annual peak or lasting trend.
- Planned nuclear testing and policy ambition are not operating outcomes.
