## Critical corrections

- Bridge deck elevation, structural height and span are different measurements.
- One tariff-incidence estimate does not permanently settle who bore all costs.
- A first freight service is not necessarily direct, unsubsidized or durable.
- State-media presence, paid promotion and coordinated inauthentic behavior are
  distinct platform phenomena.
- IP-address geography does not establish a cyberattack’s operator or sponsor.
- Tiangong-2 is not the later modular Tiangong station.
- A technical degree does not make an official a practicing scientist.
- Missing AIS, an outage or a public investigation does not by itself prove the
  strongest alleged conduct or cause.
- A source’s server location does not establish who received, used or accessed
  transmitted data.
- A 2030 lunar landing remains a program target.
