## Corrections and review posture

- The Xinjiang detention link uses the publisher's actual headline in the data,
  not the more categorical supplied paraphrase.
- The Hong Kong Free Press link is normalized from its obsolete domain to the
  current publisher URL.
- Early-event stories remain dated historical records: later outcomes do not
  retroactively become claims the original article made.
- Forecasts and proposals remain forecasts and proposals until observed data or
  enacted text establishes a later outcome.
- Allegations remain attributed even when a later conviction exists; the later
  record must be separately sourced.
- The tribunal is described by institutional status rather than allowed to
  borrow the authority of a state court.
- Countrywide generalizations cannot be built from a price anecdote, isolated
  crash stories, user reports, or viral reactions.
