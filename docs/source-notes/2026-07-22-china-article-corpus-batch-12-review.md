## Critical corrections

- A conditional statement about regulatory access is not evidence of an
  installed backdoor.
- A targeted service or content action is not a ban on an entire medium.
- A modeled cumulative waste projection is not an observed pile of waste.
- Restrictions on regulated financial institutions are not identical to a ban
  on individual possession or every transaction.
- Geospatial offsets, GPS readings and displayed map tiles are related but not
  interchangeable concepts.
- Nameplate electricity capacity is not generation, dispatchability or
  emissions.
- An online slogan or discourse is not a measured population behavior.
- A preliminary aviation agreement is not a completed production program.
- A study estimate, rating opinion or market-causality account remains labeled
  as such.
