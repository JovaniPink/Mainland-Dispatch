## Critical corrections

- A university research release is labeled as such and cannot serve as
  independent corroboration of its own paper.
- A cyber analyst's explicit warning that attribution allegations are
  unverified is preserved as a controlling limitation.
- Prototype design speed is not commercial operating speed.
- Administrative group-management duties are not rewritten as automatic
  criminal liability.
- A photograph establishes what is visible, not disputed intelligence claims
  about purpose or capability.
- Private or unnamed-source cyber attribution remains attributed rather than
  presented as public technical proof.
- A prearranged facial-recognition demonstration is not a performance benchmark.
- Country, diaspora and fleet labels never substitute for actor-level evidence.
