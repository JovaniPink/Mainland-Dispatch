## Critical corrections

- Two discussion-only submissions are absent from the catalog by design.
- A committee expert citing credible reports is not the same as a formal finding
  by every part of the United Nations.
- The 15–30% Apple figure described exploration, not completed relocation.
- An email endpoint, vendor location or corporate ownership does not establish
  state direction or surveillance.
- China’s announced 2018 easing of the rhino and tiger product ban was postponed
  weeks later; the old ban remained in force.
- A Tsinghua school advisory-board role is not leadership of the university.
- A survey response about considering emigration is not observed migration.
- Market capitalization of suspended shares is not an equivalent amount of cash
  frozen in accounts.
- A forecast, private-market superlative or lawsuit allegation remains labeled
  as such until later evidence changes its status.
